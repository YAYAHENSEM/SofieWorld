Get To Know My Hometown

This is my hometown, Kuala Kangsar also known as KK. I raised in this small city since I was born. Here is a short clip about popular places in Kuala Kangsar.

KK is famous with its "Labu Sayong". It is one of Malay heritage crafts. This pottery craft has its own special feature. The water kept inside the clay pitcher is colder than usual and is said to be refreshing to the body when consumed. Let's take a look on how to make the clay pitcher.

The Talang Cannon is a type of homemade firecracker that is popular during the Hari Raya festival season in Malaysia. It is a sign to start a new month , Syawal. The Talang Cannon was named after the original place where this cannon was played, namely in Kampung Talang, Kuala Kangsar Perak.

What is attractive, famous and special food in Kuala Kangsar? YESSS !! Obviously the laksa and cendol
